projecting.life 101

Dear ,

You are invited to Project your Life with us, Gijs and Elliott, on Apr 22 14-17h at Extra Practice.

Projecting Life, you may ask? Sometimes life is just too full of things – what we call a Project Life, while other times it's consumed by one thing – a Life Project. In both Life-Types you can get lost; in both you may want to be in the other.

To help find our route through all of this we take cues from climbing. When you fall from a rock, you can do two things: leave it or project it: committing to trying it, for an hour, day, month, until you're up. Whatever your Life-Type, we'll help each other Project our Lives.

Apr 22 will be the first practice session at XP, to be continued at Fontainebleau.
